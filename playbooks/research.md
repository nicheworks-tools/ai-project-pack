# Research Playbook

## When to use
Use when claims may affect truth, decisions, or user-facing promises and require evidence quality checks.

## What to read first
- `sources.md`
- `current-truth.md`
- `pending.md`
- `adapters/claude.md` (for evidence separation format)

## Likely files to update
- `sources.md`
- `pending.md` (if a claim remains unresolved)
- `current-truth.md` (only after support is recorded)
- `updates/<date>-*.md`

## Good mini-example
"Add a primary source citation, mark one claim as supported, and move only that claim into current-truth."

## Bad mini-example / failure mode
"Treat internal intuition as evidence and promote unresolved claims into truth."
