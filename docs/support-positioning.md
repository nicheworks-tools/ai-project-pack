# Support Positioning

`aiprojectpack` support is about operational reliability, not managed execution.

## What support covers
- improving clarity and coherence of core operating files
- strengthening examples so first-time users can run the loop successfully
- tightening validation checks to prevent regressions
- preserving public-facing quality and consistency across docs

## What support does not cover
- running your project on your behalf
- guaranteeing factual correctness of your domain content
- replacing your legal/security/compliance review

## Why support matters
Teams adopting this pack need confidence that the operating model is stable, understandable, and maintained. Strong support keeps the pack trustworthy as a public tool and reduces first-run failure rates.
